Adam and Eve's Resort

Activities

Hiking Out Nature Trails

Adam and Eve's Resort rests peacefully below over five miles of walking and hiking trails, serving as a top destination for avid outdoor enthusiasts. With comfortable benches lining the trails, guests can stop to sit, rest and enjoy the soft, natural sounds of the forest. From an adventurous and rustic Big Tree Loop trail to the longer Huckleberry Hillclimb trail, there is a route for every age and skill level. Continue your adventure along our meandering boardwalks on Licorice Fern and marvel at the Pacific Northwest rain forest along Dogwood Ridge.

Geocaching

Experience the high-tech treasure hunt known as Geocaching as you explore Adam and Eve's Resort and search for hidden containers leading to prizes.

Start the adventure at our Activities desk from 10AM-4PM with a 10 minute orientation.
